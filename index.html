"Story"

A tale of commitment and persistence.

Ever since I started exploring how websites and apps work, I knew I wanted to build them myself. I'm Anton Bertillo, a recent Bachelor of Science in Information Technology graduate from PHINMA University of Pangasinan. During my studies, I developed a strong interest in front-end development and UI/UX design creating clean, user-friendly interfaces became something I genuinely enjoyed.

Along the way, I gained hands-on experience with tools and technologies like Java, HTML, CSS, Firebase, and Python. I also explored mobile development, which deepened my understanding of how users interact with digital products on different platforms.

Now, as I take my first steps into the professional world, I'm excited to apply what I've learned, grow my skills, and contribute to projects that make a real impact.
